Feeling Lucky?

  • High Concept: Are you feeling lucky enough to save mankind from the Demon King?
  • Player Experience Goals: Players can experience testing their luck and move toward defeating the enemy.
  • Genre: The genre is adventure RPG.
  • Game play: The player uses the arrow keys to move the hero. When the hero comes in contact with enemy knights, the hero defeats the enemy, loses health, and gains levels. If a bomb is touched for too long, it will explode and deal damage to the hero. The chests contain items, a trap, or even nothing. Once the hero reaches the castle, they attack the demon king. If the hero is too weak, they are sent back to the begining, but the Demon King takes damage equal to Hero's level, but the hero also takes damage. The hero wins if the Demon King is defeated and loses if their health reaches zero.
  • Prototype goal: This game prototype uses alot of random number generator and object collision.
  • Features: This game is mainly random number generater based.
  • Setting/Premise: The Demon King wants to take over the world. The player, who plays as the hero, wants to prevent the Demon King from killing their family/friends/etc.
  • Story: The Demon King is slowly taking over the world and the human race is in trouble. As a member of the human race, you journey to the Demon King's castle hoping that you will become the hero that will save humanity.
  • Target Audience: This game is targeted toward people who enjoy classic RPG games.
  • Play time: It takes less than a minute to play this game.
  • Strategies: Players can determine when they are strong enough to fight the demon king or sacrifice some health to gain strength.
  • Prototype Assets: Graphics are taken from RPGMaker and audio is from open source websites.
